Lead Shielding

Lead shielding is being done for the safety of the radiology room. The safety assessmentis relayed upon for this shielding.We must set the shielding in accordance with this. One ofthe most used materials for shielding against radiation is lead. Lead has a relatively high density, which makes it particularly good in reducing gamma and x-ray radiation exposure. Lead shielding is utilized in a wide range of industries, including healthcare, nuclear energy, research, security, and many others, due to its effective shielding qualities and affordable pricing.
